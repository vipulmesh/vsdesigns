Standards

GD&T

Geometric Dimensioning and Tolerancing (GD&T) is a symbolic language used on engineering drawings to define the allowable variation in part geometry. It ensures design intent, manufacturability, and functional assembly across industries.

Key Topics

  • Fundamentals of GD&T Symbols
  • Datums & Feature Control Frames
  • Form, Orientation, and Location Tolerances
  • Practical Applications in Engineering Drawings
